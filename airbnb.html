How capable are Europeans of paying for accommodations?

Prices of Airbnb in Europe

This study examines the economic capability of Europeans to afford accommodations within their own capitals, focusing on the disparities in purchasing power and accommodation costs across EU and EFTA countries. Utilizing data from Eurostat and Airbnb, we integrated economic indicators with real-time accommodation prices to provide a comprehensive analysis. The findings indicate a strong correlation between GDP per capita and accommodation prices, with wealthier countries generally exhibiting higher prices. Significant disparities were observed in the ability of citizens from different countries to afford domestic tourism, with Southern and Eastern European nations facing the greatest challenges.
